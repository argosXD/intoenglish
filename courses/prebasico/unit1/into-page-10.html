P-10 SUBJECTS AND VERB TO BE

Identifying SUBJECTS pronouns


I    YOU    HE    SHE    IT    WE    THEY
Noun Subject Pronoun Predicate
My doctor = She or He He’s at the hospital
My school = It It’s huge.
My classmates and I = We We’re 20 years.
The women =They They’re gorgeous.
The socks = They They’re dirty.
A) Change the subject noun(s) to subject pronouns.

EXAMPLES:
My father is tall. He is tall.
My mother is short. She is short.
You and your friend are shy. You are shy.
My uncle is married. He is married.
